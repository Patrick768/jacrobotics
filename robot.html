Our Robot

Some would say this is the most important part of The Competition

This year to ensure our robot could handle the task we choose to build it out of extruded aluminum, or T slottedbars. This allowed us to customize our frame easily and quickly without having to constantly drill holes through metal, but also gave us the strength to have our robot be lightweight so as to not tip over on the ramp.

Our robot uses a combination of things, including but no limited to, magic, duct tape, a prayer and unicorn hair in order to function. It will pick up five (5) footballs and throw them into the V, allowing us to (hopefully) score points.
